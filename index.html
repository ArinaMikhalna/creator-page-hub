What the service does

Connect, display, and publish your TikTok videos

Creator Page Hub helps users connect their TikTok account, view authorized profile information, display their own TikTok videos on a public creator page, and publish new videos to their own TikTok account.

  • Users authorize access through TikTok OAuth.
  • Users can display their own TikTok videos on a public creator page.
  • Users can upload and publish videos to their own TikTok account.
  • Users can revoke access at any time through TikTok settings.
  • The service is available to external users and is not an internal company tool.
TikTok video publishing

Publish to your own TikTok account

Creator Page Hub allows authenticated users to upload and publish videos to their own TikTok account through TikTok Content Posting API. All publishing actions are initiated by the authenticated user.

  • Users connect their TikTok account through TikTok Login Kit.
  • Users authorize the required TikTok permissions.
  • Users upload video files inside the platform.
  • Users review the video before publishing.
  • Users click Publish to send the video to their own TikTok account.
  • The platform does not publish content without direct user action.
Permissions

TikTok permissions we request

Each permission maps directly to a feature you use inside the platform.

user.info.basic

Used to identify the authenticated TikTok user and connect their account.

user.info.profile

Used to display the creator's authorized public profile information on their creator page.

user.info.stats

Used to show authorized creator account statistics inside the user dashboard.

video.list

Used to display the user's authorized TikTok videos on their public creator page.

video.upload

Used to upload video files selected by the authenticated user.

video.publish

Used to publish videos to the authenticated user's own TikTok account after the user clicks Publish.

We only request permissions required for the platform features. Users can revoke access at any time through their TikTok account settings.

FAQ

Common questions

Is Creator Page Hub a public service?

Yes. Creator Page Hub is a public web platform available to external users. It is not an internal company tool.

How do I connect my TikTok account?

You sign in and authorize access through TikTok Login Kit and OAuth. The platform only receives the permissions you approve.

Can I publish videos to my TikTok account?

Yes. You can upload a video file inside the platform, review it, and click Publish to send it to your own TikTok account through the TikTok Content Posting API.

Does the platform publish anything automatically?

No. The platform never publishes automatically. Every upload and publish action requires your direct action, and it only publishes to your own account.

Can I revoke access?

Yes. You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings, or by contacting us to disconnect and delete your data.

Do you sell my data?

No. We do not sell personal data, and we only share data when required to operate the service, comply with the law, or protect the service from abuse.
