FAQ

Common questions

Is Creator Page Hub a public service?

Yes. Creator Page Hub is a public web service available to external users. It is not an internal company tool.

How do I connect my TikTok account?

You sign in and authorize access through TikTok OAuth. The service only receives the permissions you approve.

What data do you access?

We only access the TikTok account information and videos you authorize through TikTok OAuth, plus the technical information needed to operate the service.

Can I revoke access?

Yes. You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings, or by contacting us to disconnect and delete your data.

Do you sell my data?

No. We do not sell personal data, and we only share data when required to operate the service, comply with the law, or protect the service from abuse.
