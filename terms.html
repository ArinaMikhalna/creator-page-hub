1. Overview

Creator Page Hub is a public web platform that allows users to connect their TikTok account, display their own TikTok videos on public creator pages, and upload and publish videos to their own TikTok account through TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Publishing to TikTok

Users may upload and publish videos to their own TikTok account through the platform.

All publishing actions must be initiated by the authenticated user.

Creator Page Hub does not publish videos automatically or without user action.

Users are responsible for the videos they upload and publish.

3. User responsibility

Users must only upload and publish content they own or have the right to use.

Users must comply with TikTok Terms of Service, TikTok Developer Terms, and TikTok Community Guidelines.

4. Acceptable use

Users may not use the service to upload, publish, display, promote, or distribute illegal, abusive, misleading, infringing, malicious, spammy, or unauthorized content.

5. TikTok content

The service only accesses TikTok content authorized by the user through TikTok OAuth.

Creator Page Hub does not claim ownership of TikTok content.

6. Account connection and removal

Users may disconnect their TikTok account at any time through TikTok settings or by contacting support.

7. Service availability

The service may be changed, suspended, or discontinued at any time.
