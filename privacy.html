1. Overview

Creator Page Hub is a public web service that helps users connect their TikTok account and display their own TikTok videos on a public creator page.

2. Data we collect

When a user connects a TikTok account, we may receive limited TikTok account information and content data authorized by the user through TikTok OAuth.

This may include:

  • Basic profile information authorized by the user.
  • TikTok videos authorized by the user.
  • Technical information needed to operate the service.

3. How we use data

We use authorized data only to:

  • connect the user's TikTok account;
  • display user-authorized TikTok videos;
  • operate and improve the service;
  • provide support.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share user data with third parties except when required to operate the service, comply with the law, or protect the service from abuse.

5. User control

Users can revoke TikTok access at any time through TikTok account settings.

Users may also contact us to request deletion of their data.

6. Data retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: smm.am.agency@gmail.com
