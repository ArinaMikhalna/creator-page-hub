1. Overview

Creator Page Hub is a public web platform that helps users connect their TikTok account, display their own TikTok videos on a public creator page, and upload and publish videos to their own TikTok account through TikTok Content Posting API.

2. TikTok data we collect

When a user connects a TikTok account, we may receive data authorized by the user through TikTok OAuth, including:

  • basic TikTok account information;
  • public profile information;
  • authorized account statistics;
  • authorized TikTok video list;
  • video upload and publishing permissions.

3. How we use TikTok data

We use TikTok data only to:

  • connect the user's TikTok account;
  • display authorized profile information;
  • display authorized TikTok videos on the user's creator page;
  • show account statistics inside the user dashboard;
  • upload videos selected by the user;
  • publish videos to the user's own TikTok account after the user clicks Publish;
  • provide support and maintain the service.

4. Publishing control

The service does not publish videos automatically. Videos are uploaded and published only after the authenticated user takes direct action inside the platform.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share TikTok data with third parties except when required to operate the service, comply with the law, or protect the service from abuse.

6. User control

Users can revoke TikTok access at any time through TikTok account settings.

Users may contact us to request deletion of their data.

7. Data retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data.

9. Contact
